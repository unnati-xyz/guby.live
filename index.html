Why?

Existing remote conferencing solutions either take a one size fits all solution or do it with plain live streaming. We think we can do better. We think this can be simpler.

In person conferences are a mix up of one-to-many sessions (talks) and many-to-many sessions (networking) and our aim with Guby is to emulate as much of that as possible while keeping things straightforward for the organizers, the speakers and the attendees.

  • Video Streaming

    Guby takes a hybrid approach to video streaming. Speaker videos will be streamed for participants during talks and the attendees themselves will be capable of joining smaller video conferences during networking sessions

  • Networking Lounge

    As the whole world goes remote, Networking is bound to change. Guby aims to take a crack at this problem using rich attendee metadata (given by the attendee) to create "tables" of conversation for folks to join and leave at will

  • Chat

    Chat is another central portion of Guby. Text is clear, crisp and thoughtful. Chat helps to capture an attendee's thought in real time. Guby has an always available embedded chat room for all the attendees to interact with each other

Ease of Use

  • Organizers

    Use it for a one of conference or setup recurring meetups with just a few clicks. Almost everything that an organizer needs, is taken care of out of the box - speaker management, track support and a lounge

  • Attendees

    Join any event with just a single click. Interact with other participants as you watch the talks or jump on a discussion with other peers

  • Speakers

    No cumbersome experience in giving your talk. Simply login to the website and start talking. Full simplicity, no mercy.
