Why?

Video confereincing applications do not do justice in delivering true conference experience. Either they are one way communication or filled with chaos! We wanted to fix this and here we are with guby!

Ease of Use

  • Organizers

    Easily start conference, recurring meetups with just few clicks. Frictionless management of speakers, tracks and lounge mode.

  • Attendees

    Join any event with just one click. You just don't watch video stream, but can actively interact with other participants & speakers with networking lounges & discussion rooms.

  • Speakers

    No cumbersome setup required to share screen, stream & record content. No installation required. Login & start talking!
